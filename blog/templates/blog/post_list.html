Korea – Yonsei Rivalry 2017


The Korea-Yonsei rivalry is the college rivalry between two universities located in Seoul, South Korea. Both schools are regarded as the most prestigious private universities in South Korea.

Since 1956, annual Korea-Yonsei games has served as the most hotly contested collegiate rivalries in the country. Five sports teams: football, rugby, baseball, basketball, and ice hockey teams compete against each other.
